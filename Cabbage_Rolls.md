# Cabbage Rolls 

## Ingredients 
- Savoy Cabbage
- Ground pork 
- Onions (white or yellow)
- Scallions 
- Shiitake mushrooms (can be dried)  
- Shaoxing wine  
- Cooked white rice  
- Garlic and ginger  
- Egg 
- Soy sauce 
- salt  
 

## Instructions
1. Steam savoy cabbage
3. Mix meat ingredients and wrap into cabbage like a burrito 
2. Steam for 15 mins
